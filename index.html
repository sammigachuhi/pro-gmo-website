About Us

We are a group of GMO experts (geneticists) and non-experts (like you and me) who are proponents for advancement and mainstreaming of safe, healthy and approved Genetically Modified Organisms (GMOs).

  • Safety to us means the GMOs pose no harm
  • Healthy to us means that GMOs are okayed for human consumption
  • Approved means that the product has passed through all the necessary tests and regulations

GMOs are the largely unexplored frontier of the future. We do not believe in overruning the world with GMOs, but in creating a world where GMOs can co-exist harmoniously with nature. We advocate for use of GMOs where they are the most reasonable alternative.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Genetically Modified Organisms?

By definition, a GMO, or genetically modified organism, is a plant, animal or another organism that has had its genetic makeup modified in order to produce a more desirable outcome, such as enhanced nutrition or a stronger resistance to disease (GMO Answers)

Are GMO foods safe to eat?

GMO foods are as healthful and safe to eat as their non-GMO counterparts. Some GMO plants have actually been modified to improve their nutritional value. (US F.D.A)
